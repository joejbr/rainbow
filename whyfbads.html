First of all, why Facebook Ads?

You want good patients to show up to your office, and you don’t want to overspend on advertising to get them.
If you make a million dollars but you spend $999k to make it, I don’t think that’s a good idea…
Now let’s imagine you live in San Diego for example and you decided to promote your practice with billboard advertising, that will cost you between $11,000 to $23,000 per month.
So you might be thingking that you’re paying for all these people in these cars to see your advertisement.
But the truth is only a small 1% are a potential customer.
This is true of all print, tv, radio and magazine advertising.
You pay for everyone to see your ad, regardless if they are likely to become a patient or not!
Essentially, it can really be a waste of money! With no good results.

Facebook Ads are different. You can actually choose who you show your ads to.
So if you target your ideal customer, then you only pay when your ideal customer sees your ad!
How awesome is that!

What this means for results?
Since we are only showing our ads to our ideal customer,
we are getting much more for our money.
We can spend very little, and get a lot in return.
